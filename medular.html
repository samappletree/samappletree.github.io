UCI Health Operating Room(OR) Management Application

Senior Design Project: Sep 2016 – Mar 2017(6 months)

Role: Researcher, UI Designer, Front-End Developer, Project Manager (Team: 5 members)

Overview

This project was for my senior design course at the University of California, Irvine. We were partnered up with Dr. Govind Rajan, an Anesthesiologist from the UCI Medical Center.

Medular is a highly scalable web application that allows OR Managers to provision and de-provision departmental personnel accordingly. The application offers a birds-eye view of the entire operating room infrastructure with the capability to record profile-specific user-input to make changes to the virtualized “grease board” as well as manage employee tasks and breaks.

The Problems

Our Project Sponsor came to us with a set of multiple problems he was currently facing in his operating rooms:

  • Scheduling Operating Room Staff is hard
  • Multiple OR Scheduling Softwares in use
  • Lack of Proper Task Assignments, especially related to handling employee breaks
  • Constant Phone Communication

The Research

In order to create a solution for Dr. Rajan and his staff, my team needed to gain a better understanding of their current workflow and the problems they face on a daily basis.

We began our research by getting a tour of their facilities and interviewing available staff members.

Interviews and Observations

We spent countless hours and sessions communicating with Dr. Rajan and his fellow staff members to understand their daily work flow.
